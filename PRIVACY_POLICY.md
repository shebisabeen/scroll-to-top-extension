# Privacy Policy for Scroll to Top Extension

**Last Updated:** March 9, 2026

## Overview

Scroll to Top Extension ("the Extension") is a simple Chrome browser extension that adds a convenient scroll-to-top button to web pages. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any user data.**

The Scroll to Top Extension:

- ❌ Does **not** collect any personal information
- ❌ Does **not** track your browsing history or activity
- ❌ Does **not** store any data on external servers
- ❌ Does **not** use cookies or any tracking technologies
- ❌ Does **not** share any information with third parties
- ❌ Does **not** transmit any data over the internet

## How the Extension Works

The Extension operates entirely within your browser. It simply injects a scroll-to-top button into web pages you visit, allowing you to quickly return to the top of a page with a single click. All functionality is local and self-contained.

## Permissions

The Extension requests only the minimum permissions necessary to function:

- **Active Tab / Content Scripts:** Used solely to display the scroll-to-top button on web pages.

No permission is used to collect, transmit, or store any user data.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

If we make any changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document. Since we do not collect data, any changes will only reflect clarifications to this policy.

## Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/shebisabeen/scroll-to-top-extension).
